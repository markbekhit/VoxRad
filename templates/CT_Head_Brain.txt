### Exam:
CT Head and Brain

### Technique:
Axial cuts were obtained from the skull base to the vertex. Bone window and soft tissue window reconstructions were reviewed. [If contrast: Scans were acquired pre- and post-intravenous contrast administration.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- Record clinical details exactly as dictated. Do not rephrase or reformulate the wording.
- **Exclude** patient name, date of birth, age, gender, and MRN/accession numbers from the report, even if mentioned in the transcript.
- **If not provided:** 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** List the prior imaging studies mentioned (modality and date/year if stated). Do not summarise changes here — comparison findings belong in the Findings section.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior imaging available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each section: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use an appropriate normal descriptor.
- Normal groups may be combined into a single statement. Only itemise individually when pathology is present.

**Brain Parenchyma**
- Default: "Brain parenchyma unremarkable. No intracranial haemorrhage, infarct, mass lesion, or abnormal density."
- If pathology: describe density (hyper/hypo), location, size, mass effect, and midline shift.

**White Matter**
- Default: "White matter unremarkable. No focal low-density lesion."

**Basal Ganglia and Thalami**
- Default: "Basal ganglia and thalami unremarkable."

**Posterior Fossa**
- Default: "Cerebellum and brainstem unremarkable."

**Ventricular System**
- Default: "Ventricles normal in size and configuration. No hydrocephalus."

**Sulci and Basal Cisterns**
- Default: "Sulci and basal cisterns patent. No sulcal effacement or transtentorial herniation."

**Extra-Axial Spaces**
- Default: "No subdural, extradural, or subarachnoid haemorrhage. No extra-axial collection."

**Skull and Calvarium**
- Default: "Skull vault intact. No fracture."

**Paranasal Sinuses and Mastoids**
- Default: "Paranasal sinuses clear. Mastoid air cells well aerated."

**Orbits**
- Default: "Orbits unremarkable (to the extent visualised)."

**Soft Tissues**
- Default: "Extracranial soft tissues unremarkable."

### Impression:
**Instructions:**
- Concise bullet-point summary.
- Include only clinically relevant findings. Minor incidental findings that are fully described in the body do not need to be restated here.
- For a normal/negative study, include a closing statement where appropriate, e.g. 'No cause for the patient's symptoms identified on this examination.'

  - No intracranial haemorrhage, mass lesion, or midline shift.
