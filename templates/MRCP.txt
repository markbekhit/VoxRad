### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- Localise biliary findings by segment (intrahepatic vs extrahepatic) and named duct (right/left hepatic, common hepatic, cystic, common bile duct).
- Measure the common bile duct at the porta hepatis and the pancreatic duct at the head, body, and tail.

**Liver**
- Default: "Normal liver size, contour, and signal. No focal hepatic lesion."

**Intrahepatic Bile Ducts**
- Default: "Intrahepatic bile ducts not dilated. No filling defect or stricture."
- If dilated: quantify calibre, identify level of obstruction, and describe any mass, stricture morphology (smooth, irregular, shouldered), or filling defect.

**Extrahepatic Bile Ducts (CHD and CBD)**
- Default: "Common hepatic and common bile ducts normal in calibre (CBD < 7 mm) with smooth walls and no filling defect or stricture."

**Gallbladder and Cystic Duct**
- Default: "Gallbladder normal in size and wall thickness. No calculus, sludge, or wall thickening. Cystic duct patent, no calculus."
- If gallstones: number (single/multiple), size range, and presence of acute cholecystitis (wall thickening > 3 mm, pericholecystic fluid, gallbladder wall oedema).

**Pancreas and Pancreatic Duct**
- Default: "Pancreas normal in size, contour, and signal. Pancreatic duct normal calibre (< 3 mm), smooth, and non-dilated. No focal mass or cyst."

**Portal Venous System**
- Default: "Portal vein, splenic vein, and superior mesenteric vein are patent."

**Spleen, Adrenals, Kidneys**
- Default: "Spleen, adrenal glands, and kidneys are unremarkable."

**Ampulla of Vater / Periampullary Region**
- Default: "No ampullary mass or periampullary abnormality."

**Other**
- Default: "No ascites, lymphadenopathy, or peritoneal nodularity."
