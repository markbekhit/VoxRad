### Exam:
CT Abdomen and Pelvis

### Technique:
Axial cuts were obtained from the dome of the diaphragm to the pubic symphysis. Coronal and sagittal reformatting were then obtained. [If contrast: Scans were acquired in the [pre-contrast / arterial / portal venous / delayed] phase following intravenous contrast administration.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:**
  - 'The patient presented with [describe symptoms or reason for the examination], with a medical history notable for [mention relevant medical conditions].'
  - **Exclude** name, age, gender, and IDs anywhere in the report, even if provided in the transcript.

- **If not provided:**
  - 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** Mention any prior imaging studies and briefly note significant changes or findings compared to previous examinations.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior imaging scans/reports available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each section: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use an appropriate normal descriptor.
- Normal groups may be combined into a single statement. Only itemise individually when pathology is present.
- For masses: describe size, attenuation, enhancement pattern, and relationship to adjacent structures.
- Consolidate similar findings across locations into a single statement.

**Liver**
- Default: "Liver normal in size and attenuation. No focal hepatic lesion."

**Gallbladder and Biliary System**
- Default: "Gallbladder unremarkable. No cholelithiasis or wall thickening. Biliary system not dilated."

**Spleen**
- Default: "Spleen normal in size and attenuation. No focal splenic lesion."

**Pancreas**
- Default: "Pancreas unremarkable. No focal lesion or ductal dilatation."

**Adrenal Glands**
- Default: "Adrenal glands unremarkable bilaterally."

**Kidneys and Ureters**
- Default: "Kidneys normal in size and enhancement. No hydronephrosis, calculus, or focal renal lesion. Ureters not dilated."

**Bowel**
- Default: "Small and large bowel loops unremarkable. No obstruction, wall thickening, or perforation. No free air."

**Mesentery and Peritoneum**
- Default: "Mesentery and peritoneum unremarkable. No ascites."

**Abdominal Aorta and IVC**
- Default: "Abdominal aorta normal in calibre. IVC unremarkable."

**Lymph Nodes**
- Default: "No significant abdominal or pelvic lymphadenopathy."

**Urinary Bladder**
- Default: "Urinary bladder unremarkable."

**Pelvic Organs**
- Default: "Uterus and ovaries unremarkable." [or "Prostate unremarkable." in male patients]

**Pelvic Sidewalls and Soft Tissues**
- Default: "Pelvic sidewalls and soft tissues unremarkable."

**Bony Pelvis and Lumbar Spine**
- ALWAYS use the radiologist's exact dictated words for bony findings. Only use the default "Bony pelvis and visualised lumbar spine intact. No lytic or sclerotic lesion." if the radiologist said NOTHING about bones.
