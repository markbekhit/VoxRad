### Exam:
MRI Brain

### Technique:
MRI of the brain was performed using a standard brain protocol including T1-weighted, T2-weighted, FLAIR, DWI/ADC, and GRE/SWI sequences in axial, coronal, and sagittal planes. [If contrast: Post-gadolinium T1-weighted sequences were also obtained.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- Record clinical details exactly as dictated. Do not rephrase or reformulate the wording.
- **Exclude** patient name, date of birth, age, gender, and MRN/accession numbers from the report, even if mentioned in the transcript.
- **If not provided:** 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** List the prior imaging studies mentioned (modality and date/year if stated). Do not summarise changes here — comparison findings belong in the Findings section.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior imaging available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each section: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use an appropriate normal descriptor.
- Describe signal characteristics (T1/T2/FLAIR/DWI), enhancement pattern, size, and mass effect where applicable.
- Normal groups may be combined into a single statement.

**Cerebral Hemispheres**
- Default: "No abnormal T2/FLAIR signal in the cerebral hemispheres. No focal parenchymal lesion."
- Describe lobar location, signal characteristics, and mass effect if pathology is present.

**Posterior Fossa**
- Default: "Cerebellum and brainstem unremarkable. No posterior fossa mass or signal abnormality."

**Basal Ganglia and Thalami**
- Default: "Basal ganglia and thalami unremarkable."

**Diffusion-Weighted Imaging**
- Default: "No restricted diffusion to suggest acute infarction."

**Susceptibility-Weighted Imaging**
- Default: "No haemorrhagic focus or abnormal susceptibility."

**Ventricular System**
- Default: "Ventricles normal in size and configuration. No hydrocephalus."

**Sulci and Cisterns**
- Default: "Sulci and basal cisterns patent. No sulcal effacement."

**Extra-Axial Spaces**
- Default: "No subdural or extradural collection. No subarachnoid haemorrhage."

**Midline Structures**
- Default: "Corpus callosum intact. No midline shift."

**Pituitary Gland and Sella**
- Default: "Pituitary gland unremarkable."

**Internal Auditory Canals and Cerebellopontine Angles**
- Default: "Internal auditory canals and cerebellopontine angles unremarkable."

**Skull Base and Calvarium**
- Default: "Skull base and calvarium unremarkable."

**Extracranial Soft Tissues**
- Default: "Extracranial soft tissues unremarkable."

[correct spellings] MRI brain. No abnormal T2 or FLAIR signal. No acute infarct on DWI. No intracranial haemorrhage. Ventricles normal in size and configuration. No midline shift. Corpus callosum intact. Posterior fossa unremarkable. Pituitary gland normal. No extra-axial collection. Skull base intact. [correct spellings]
- If 'query' is mentioned in the transcript, it should be rendered as '?' in the report.

### Impression:
**Instructions:**
- Concise bullet-point summary.
- Include only clinically relevant findings. Minor incidental findings that are fully described in the body do not need to be restated here.
- For a normal/negative study, include a closing statement where appropriate, e.g. 'No cause for the patient's symptoms identified on this examination.'
- **Sample:**
  - No acute intracranial abnormality on MRI brain.
