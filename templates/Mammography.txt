### Exam:
Mammography [Screening / Diagnostic] — Bilateral

### Technique:
Bilateral digital mammography was performed in standard CC (craniocaudal) and MLO (mediolateral oblique) projections. [If tomosynthesis used: Digital breast tomosynthesis (3D) was also acquired.] [If diagnostic: Additional spot compression / magnification / true lateral views of the [right/left] [quadrant] were obtained.] [If US correlation: Targeted ultrasound of the [right/left] breast was performed — see separate report.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- Record clinical details exactly as dictated. Do not rephrase or reformulate the wording.
- **Exclude** patient name, date of birth, age, gender, and MRN/accession numbers from the report, even if mentioned in the transcript.
- **If not provided:** 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** List the prior mammograms referenced (year and facility if stated). Comparison findings (stability, interval change) belong in the Findings section.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior mammograms available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- Use standard BI-RADS lexicon for composition and abnormality descriptors. Spell out side (right/left) — do not abbreviate.
- Localise all findings by side, quadrant (UOQ, UIQ, LOQ, LIQ, retroareolar, subareolar), depth (anterior/middle/posterior third), and clock face position where relevant.

**Breast Composition (ACR BI-RADS)**
- Default: State one of:
  - a. The breasts are almost entirely fatty.
  - b. There are scattered areas of fibroglandular density.
  - c. The breasts are heterogeneously dense, which may obscure small masses.
  - d. The breasts are extremely dense, which lowers the sensitivity of mammography.

**Masses**
- Default: "No discrete mass identified."
- If present: describe by shape (oval/round/irregular), margin (circumscribed/obscured/microlobulated/indistinct/spiculated), and density (high/equal/low/fat-containing). Include size in mm and location.

**Calcifications**
- Default: "No suspicious microcalcifications."
- If present: distinguish benign-appearing (vascular, skin, coarse/popcorn, rim, dystrophic, milk of calcium, suture) from suspicious (amorphous, coarse heterogeneous, fine pleomorphic, fine linear/linear branching). Describe distribution (diffuse, regional, grouped/clustered, linear, segmental).

**Architectural Distortion**
- Default: "No architectural distortion."

**Asymmetries**
- Default: "No asymmetry, focal asymmetry, global asymmetry, or developing asymmetry."

**Associated Features**
- Default: "No skin thickening, nipple retraction, skin retraction, trabecular thickening, or axillary lymphadenopathy."

**Implants (if applicable)**
- Comment on implant type (saline/silicone), position (subglandular/subpectoral), and integrity. If no implants: omit this section.

[correct spellings] mammography, tomosynthesis, craniocaudal, mediolateral oblique, fibroglandular, heterogeneously dense, extremely dense, BI-RADS, microcalcifications, pleomorphic, amorphous, coarse heterogeneous, architectural distortion, spiculated, circumscribed, microlobulated, indistinct margins, focal asymmetry, developing asymmetry, trabecular thickening, axillary lymphadenopathy, subglandular, subpectoral, saline, silicone, implant integrity, intracapsular rupture, extracapsular rupture. [correct spellings]
- If 'query' is mentioned in the transcript, it should be rendered as '?' in the report.

### Impression:
**Instructions:**
- Concise bullet-point summary. Always end with a BI-RADS category for each breast (or a single combined category if symmetric).
- BI-RADS assessment categories:
  - 0 — Incomplete; additional imaging and/or prior comparison needed.
  - 1 — Negative.
  - 2 — Benign.
  - 3 — Probably benign (short-interval follow-up).
  - 4 — Suspicious (4A low, 4B moderate, 4C high).
  - 5 — Highly suggestive of malignancy.
  - 6 — Known biopsy-proven malignancy.
- **Sample:**
  - No suspicious mass, calcification, or architectural distortion.
  - BI-RADS Category 1 — Negative. Recommend routine screening mammography in 12 months.
