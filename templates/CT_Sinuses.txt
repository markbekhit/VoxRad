**Instructions:**

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each sinus/group: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use the default.
- When opacification is present, describe as mucosal thickening (thickness in mm), polypoid/retention cyst, complete opacification, or air-fluid level. Localise anatomic obstruction (ostiomeatal complex, sphenoethmoidal recess, frontal recess).

**Frontal Sinuses**
- Default: "Frontal sinuses clear and well aerated."

**Ethmoid Air Cells (Anterior and Posterior)**
- Default: "Anterior and posterior ethmoid air cells clear."

**Maxillary Sinuses**
- Default: "Maxillary sinuses clear. Ostiomeatal complexes patent bilaterally."

**Sphenoid Sinuses**
- Default: "Sphenoid sinuses clear. Sphenoethmoidal recesses patent."

**Nasal Cavity and Septum**
- Default: "Nasal septum midline. Turbinates unremarkable. No nasal polyp or mass."

**Osseous Structures**
- Default: "No bony erosion or aggressive osseous lesion. No fracture."

**Anatomic Variants**
- Comment on variants relevant to endoscopic sinus surgery: concha bullosa, Haller cell, Onodi cell, septal deviation, dehiscence of the lamina papyracea or cribriform plate, Keros classification of the olfactory fossa.

**Orbits and Adjacent Structures**
- Default: "Orbits and visualised intracranial structures unremarkable."
