### Exam:
CT Neck

### Technique:
Axial CT images of the neck were obtained from the skull base to the thoracic inlet. Coronal and sagittal reformats were reviewed. [If contrast: Scans were acquired following intravenous contrast administration.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- Record clinical details exactly as dictated. Do not rephrase or reformulate the wording.
- **Exclude** patient name, date of birth, age, gender, and MRN/accession numbers from the report, even if mentioned in the transcript.
- **If not provided:** 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** List the prior imaging studies mentioned (modality and date/year if stated). Do not summarise changes here — comparison findings belong in the Findings section.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior imaging available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each section: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use an appropriate normal descriptor — do NOT write "appears normal" generically; use precise radiology language.
- Localise nodal disease by level (I–VI) and side. Report pathologic nodes by short-axis diameter.

**Nasopharynx and Oropharynx**
- Default: "Nasopharynx and oropharynx symmetric and unremarkable. No mucosal mass."

**Hypopharynx and Larynx**
- Default: "Hypopharynx, supraglottis, glottis, and subglottis are symmetric. No mucosal lesion or airway narrowing. Piriform sinuses and valleculae are clear."

**Oral Cavity and Floor of Mouth**
- Default: "Oral cavity, tongue base, and floor of mouth unremarkable."

**Salivary Glands**
- Default: "Parotid, submandibular, and sublingual glands symmetric and normal in attenuation. No focal mass or ductal dilatation. No calculus."

**Thyroid**
- Default: "Thyroid gland symmetric, homogeneous in attenuation, with no focal nodule or mass."

**Cervical Lymph Nodes**
- Default: "No enlarged cervical lymph nodes. No pathologically enlarged node by size or morphologic criteria."
- If pathologic: localise by level (Ia, Ib, II, III, IV, Va, Vb, VI, retropharyngeal) and side; give short-axis diameter; note central necrosis or extracapsular extension.

**Major Vessels**
- Default: "Carotid arteries and jugular veins are patent with no filling defect or aneurysm."

**Cervical Spine and Skull Base**
- Default: "Visualised cervical spine and skull base unremarkable. No destructive bony lesion."

**Lung Apices**
- Default: "Visualised lung apices clear."

### Impression:
**Instructions:**
- Concise bullet-point summary of key findings.
- Include only clinically relevant findings. Minor incidental findings that are fully described in the body do not need to be restated here.
- For a normal/negative study, include a closing statement where appropriate, e.g. 'No cause for the patient's symptoms identified on this examination.'
- **Sample:**
  - No aerodigestive mucosal mass or significant cervical lymphadenopathy.
  - Thyroid and salivary glands unremarkable.
