### THIS IS THE SAMPLE TEMPLATE TO BE USED FOR THE TRANSCRIPT ###

---

### Exam:
Chest X-Ray (CXR)

### Technique:
[PA / AP / Lateral] projection chest radiograph was obtained. [Portable AP if bedside.]

### Clinical Details
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:**
  - 'The patient presented with [describe symptoms or reason for the examination], with a medical history notable for [mention relevant medical conditions].'
  - **Exclude** name, age, gender, and IDs anywhere in the report, even if provided in the transcript.

- **If not provided:**
  - 'No clinical details provided.'

### Comparison:
**Instructions:**
- **If provided:** Mention any prior imaging studies and briefly note significant changes or findings compared to previous examinations.
- **If not provided:** 'No prior imaging scans/reports available for comparison.'

### Findings:
**Instructions:**
- Present findings using the bold section headers below in this exact order. Do NOT create new headings or rename sections.
- For each section: if the radiologist mentioned it, reproduce their exact words. If not mentioned, use an appropriate normal descriptor.
- Normal groups may be combined. Only itemise individually when pathology is present.

**Technical Adequacy**
- Comment on rotation, inspiratory effort, and exposure if suboptimal. Omit this section entirely if technically adequate.

**Lungs**
- Default: "Lungs clear bilaterally. No focal consolidation, collapse, or nodule."
- If pathology: describe by zone (upper/mid/lower), laterality, morphology, and distribution.

**Hila**
- Default: "Hila normal in size and density."

**Pleura**
- Default: "No pleural effusion, pneumothorax, or pleural thickening."

**Cardiac Silhouette**
- Default: "Heart normal in size. Cardiothoracic ratio within normal limits."

**Mediastinum**
- Default: "Mediastinal contour normal. Trachea central. Aortic knuckle unremarkable."

**Hemidiaphragms**
- Default: "Hemidiaphragms well-defined bilaterally. Costophrenic angles sharp."

**Bones**
- Default: "Bony thorax intact. No rib fracture or lytic lesion."
- ALWAYS use the radiologist's exact words if bones are mentioned.

**Soft Tissues**
- Default: "Soft tissues unremarkable."

**Visible Abdomen**
- Default: "No free subdiaphragmatic gas."

Lines, tubes, and devices — comment if present.

- **ASR Prompt (for speech recognition spelling correction ONLY — do NOT copy this text into the report):**
[correct spellings] chest x-ray PA. Lungs clear bilaterally, no consolidation, effusion, or pneumothorax. Hila normal. Heart normal size, cardiothoracic ratio within normal limits. Mediastinum normal. Trachea central. Costophrenic angles sharp. No rib fracture. Soft tissues unremarkable. No free air under the diaphragm. [correct spellings]
- If 'query' is mentioned in the transcript, it should be rendered as '?' in the report.

### Impression:
**Instructions:**
- Concise bullet-point summary.
- **Sample:**
  - No significant cardiopulmonary abnormality.
