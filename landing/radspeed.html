How it works

Three steps to a finished report.

01
Dictate
Press record and speak naturally. Describe findings as you read the images — no need to say section headings or formatting commands.
02
Transcribe & structure
Real-time speech-to-text captures every word. The AI engine organises findings, adds standard headers, and formats the impression.
03
Review & copy
Scan the report, make any tweaks with your voice, then copy to your RIS with one click. Done. Next case.
